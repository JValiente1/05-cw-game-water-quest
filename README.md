# charity: water – Water Quest

Water Quest is a charity: water-inspired browser game where players collect jerry cans, chase combos, and try to beat the clock.

## Features

- 30-second rounds with a 20-point goal.
- Click or keyboard-play can collection.
- Combo scoring, miss penalties, and increasing difficulty.
- Live score, combo, best score, and timer updates.
- Win/lose overlays with randomized end messages.
- charity: water branding, responsive layout, and keyboard accessibility.

## Controls

- Mouse: click the can to collect it.
- Keyboard: press `Enter` on the start button, then use `Enter` or `Space` to collect the focused can.
